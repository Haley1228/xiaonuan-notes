# 2.related work

**两个工作重点** 

Multimodal Sentiment Analysis (MSA) seeks to capture multidimensional emotional characteristics by fusing heterogeneous information sources such as text, audio, and visual modalities.
Recent advancements in this field primarily focus on stage pipeline: multimodal fusion strategies and representation learning before fusion.
Early MSA research achieved multimodal representations through feature-level fusion methods.The Tensor Fusion Network (TFN) captured high-order cross-modal interactions by computing outer products of modality-specific features.Building upon TFN, Low-rank Multimodal Fusion (LMF)reduces  costs of high-dimmensional tensors through low-rank decomposition.With the rise of Transformer architectures, attention-based fusion becomes dominant.
